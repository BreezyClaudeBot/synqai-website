The Challenge

Most businesses treat their website, phone line, forms, and follow-up as separate pieces — usually managed in different tools or by different people.

The result: inquiries fall through the gaps, response time is slow, and leads quietly go to a competitor.

What We Do

Web Design and AI Automation Under One Roof

Most businesses treat their website, phone line, forms, and follow-up process as separate pieces. SynqAI connects those pieces into a cleaner lead-capture system.

We build websites that clearly explain the business, then connect AI tools that help answer questions, capture customer details, and recover missed inquiries.

Our Approach

Three Principles Behind Every Project

Clear Design

A website should explain what the business does, why it matters, and how to take the next step. Clarity converts better than complexity.

Practical AI

AI should solve real communication problems. It should not add complexity for the sake of sounding advanced. If it does not improve inquiry flow, we do not include it.

Lead Capture First

Every system should make it easier for a business to capture, organize, and respond to inquiries. That is the measure of whether it is working.
