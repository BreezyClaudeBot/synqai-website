Missed-Call Recovery SMS

When a business misses a call, SynqAI can automatically send a follow-up SMS to the caller. This gives the business a second chance to recover the inquiry before the customer moves on.

  • Automatic SMS sent after every missed call
  • Captures customer intent via text
  • Helps businesses respond faster
  • Reduces lost leads from unanswered calls
  • Useful for clinics, contractors, and service firms
  • Works as a simple, low-friction recovery layer
